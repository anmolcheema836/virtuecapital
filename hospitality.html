Our Story

Founded by clinicians, for clinicians.

Better Way Passage was born from a simple observation: the clinicians most dedicated to patient care are often the most burdened by the business of healthcare. We saw brilliant practitioners struggling with administrative overhead, technology choices, and the isolation of private practice.

We decided to build a nonprofit to bridge that gap. BWP is a community and a resource hub, committed to making private practice sustainable and rewarding, which we believe is essential for a more equitable and effective healthcare system.

Our Values:

  • Clinician-Centric: Our work is guided by the real-world needs of practitioners.
  • Data-Driven: We use rigorous research to create tools that work.
  • Collaborative: We believe in the power of partnership to drive change.
  • Accessible: We strive to make our resources available to all who need them.
