Our Story

Hernandez, a band from the '70s, skillfully combines Anglo and Hispanic styles, reflecting their Puerto Rican roots and the influence of their guitarist father, Rafael Hernandez. In 1999, Hernandez was proud to be inducted into the Buffalo Music Hall of Fame, an honor shared with the Buffalo Philharmonic Orchestra and The Scinta Brothers, celebrating their significant musical journey.


Our History

It all began in Spanish Harlem. Starting from humble roots, we have steadily grown thanks to our unwavering dedication and constant desire to improve. We're truly passionate about our music, and we're eager to share our journey with you.
