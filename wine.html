Product Description

The Aragosta (Italian for lobster) characterizes the Vermentino wines of Cantina Santa Maria la Palma and is a classic with cult status, not only because of its label. A blend of grapes from many locations supplied by the cooperative members. It doesn't get more Sardinian than this! The Aragosta line has been one of our bestsellers for many years. [18]

The origin of the white Vermentino grape variety is unclear. It probably came from Madeira to Italy via Spain. From Gallura, it spread throughout the island around 800 AD but is mainly found in the coastal areas. In Sardinia, it is considered THE white island grape variety that produces characterful and fresh white wines. It has had DOC status since 1988. [18]
